What is healthy eating?

Healthy eating. Everybody has their own ideas on what they think it is. If you ask anyone, the most common definition you'll get from many people is having a variety of foods in the meals you eat, and in many ways they are right. according to breastcancer.org,healthy eating means eating a variety of foods that give you the nutrients you need to maintain your health, feel good and have energy. these foods should include nutrients such as carbohydrates, proteins, water, fats, vitamins, minerals, etc. this is a general idea on what healthy eating is. according to the dietitian I interviewed, Kelly Costello,MD and regestered dietitian at the ufm, some people have some misconceptions of healthy eating because what one person says it's healthy isn't necessarily going to be what another people say is healthy.


what is unhealthy eating?

As stated above basically healthy eating is eating foods with various nutrients that are beneficial to you, and unhealthy eating is basically the opposite of this. According to sahealth.sa.gov, If you don't eat healthily There areplenty of risks that are going to come to you, including stress, tiredness, obesity, tooth decay, high blood pressure, etc. Some reasons that contribute to unhealthy eating include wrong information, slightly stated above, in which one person's healthy diet is different from another person's healthy diet, which as the dietitian I interviewed mentioned that some people tend to follow "fad" diets such as low carb diets, etc.While these diets may work for some people, they are also wrong for others. Another factor for unhealthy eating is availability. According to sfgate.com, some of the foods that you would consider healthy are typically most of the time very expensive, and people are not willing to pay that much, thus are forced to pay for the cheaper unhealthy foods.
