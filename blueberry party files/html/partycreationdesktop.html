Party Creation

The Blueberry Party was formed by a small group of people with the same hopes for America. We all leaned toward the liberal left side of the spectrum. The left is usually associated with the color blue, thus inspiring our name as the “Blueberry Party”. We talked and discussed which ideas were most important to us and decided with equality for same-sex couples, gun control, supporting immigrants, reducing climate change, and providing healthcare for all Americans. We did research on each topic, made a slogan and logo, made a commercial, a website, merchandise, a presentation, and we are currently running our candidate for president.
