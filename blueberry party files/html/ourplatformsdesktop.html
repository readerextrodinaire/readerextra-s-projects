Our Platform

Makeup of Our Platform

Our Platform for is made up of five planks, namely Same-sex Marriage, Gun Control, Immigration, Climate Change, and Health Care. these are issues in todays society that we will aim to try and solve. below are our planks with research supporting our reasons, and our solution to the issue. please take your time to look around, and when done, there will be a form at the bottom of the oage to fill out, please fll out the form, we would love to see your responses!

Same-Sex Marriage

We will ensure that same sex couples have the same rights and privileges as the heterosexual couples do.
In some states in america, people doesn’t support same sex marriage. Homosexual couples can be denied full parental rights for the children. As long as the children are in a loving home with full of love and warmth, it doesn’t matter whether the parents are heterosexual or not. Homosexual couples doesn’t have a same rights as heterosexual couples.

Gun Control

We will ban assault rifles in order to keep people in America safe.
Every year guns are responsible for over 33,000 deaths in the united states. 21,386 of the 42,826 suicides in the U.S in 2014 involved a firearm. 11,008 of the 15,872 homicides in the U.S in the same year also involved a firearm. There is a lot of controversy around the 2nd Amendment in the US. Some people think it means that the government can’t regulate how many firearms you own, and say it’s is their right to bear arms. Others say that the amendment meant the national government can’t regulate the state’s right to arm their military forces.

Immigration

We will support immigrants and refugees by providing them with the same benefits as citizens.
Everyone in the america is technically an immigrant (unless you are native american), so discrimination against immigrants should not be tolerated. Banning people from certain countries from coming to America does not make sense because not everyone is a threat. It is a huge assumption to say that everyone wanting to come here is a terrorist or possible gang member. Immigrants are “makers”, not “takers”. Immigrants (including undocumented ones) pay taxes In 2010, undocumented immigrants paid about $11.2 billion dollars in state and local taxes. Immigrants are beneficial to the US economy. Instead of working to deport undocumented immigrants, we should work to make them citizens.

Health Care

We will support equal health care for every US citizens in order to help people in need
Giving every American health security, health care that's always there, health care that can never be taken away. Health care should equal to every US citizens.
